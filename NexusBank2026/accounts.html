FAQ
Common questions

Everything you need to know before opening an account.

How long does it take to open an account?
Opening an account at NexusBank takes less than 5 minutes. You'll need a valid government-issued ID, your Social Security Number, and a funding source such as an existing bank account or debit card. Once verified, your account is active immediately.
Can I have multiple account types at the same time?
Absolutely. You can open a Checking, Savings, and Investment account simultaneously and manage them all from one dashboard. Transfers between your accounts are instant and free.
Are there any monthly fees?
No. NexusBank charges zero monthly maintenance fees on all account types. There are no minimum balance requirements and no hidden charges. The only fees that may apply are for optional premium features like wire transfers to international accounts.
How is the 4.25% APY savings rate calculated?
The APY (Annual Percentage Yield) is calculated daily and credited to your savings account at the end of each calendar month. The rate is variable and subject to change, though we always aim to offer one of the most competitive rates in the market.
What happens if I suspect unauthorized activity?
Contact our 24/7 support team immediately via live chat or phone. You can also freeze your account instantly from the app with a single tap. NexusBank's fraud team will investigate and, if confirmed, will refund any unauthorized transactions within 3 business days.
